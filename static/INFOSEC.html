According to a 2006 report by email security firm Sophos, over 50% of spam is sent by remotely controlled computers infected with spyware. MySecurityIQ

The average value of a lost laptop is $49,246 and the data breach costs represent 80 percent of the total cost of a lost laptop compared to two percent for replacing the computer. Encryption on average can reduce the cost of a lost laptop by more than $20,000. The Billion Dollar Lost Laptop Study, Ponemon Institute and Intel Corp., December 2010
